Application Details & Developer

Application Name: Insurance Service (Project 5)

A comprehensive Angular application built for modern insurance management.

Developed by: Shruthi

Version: 1.0.0 (Aug 2024)

Key Features

  • Personalized Premium Calculation: Uses a series of pop-ups to calculate premium based on age, earnings, and health status.
  • Comprehensive Plan Viewing: Easily view all available insurance plans, dynamically fetched from the JSON server.
  • Secure Booking Flow: A seamless and secure process for selecting a plan, entering payment details, and confirming subscription.
  • Robust Data Validation: Includes both front-end and back-end checks (e.g., 10-digit phone, non-negative age) to ensure data integrity.

Guide to Using the Application

  1. Click "Get a Quote" to enter your personal and health data for immediate premium calculation.
  2. View the updated "View Plans" page to see plans with your personalized premium amounts.
  3. Click "Select Plan" on your preferred insurance package to begin the booking process.
  4. Complete the Booking Form with your contact and payment details securely.
  5. Click "Book Now" to finalize the purchase and receive a success confirmation.
