Guide to Use the Application

  1. Click **"Get a Quote"** to enter your personal and health data for premium calculation.
  2. View the updated **"View Plans"** page showing personalized premium amounts.
  3. Click **"Select Plan"** on your preferred insurance plan.
  4. Complete the **Booking Form** with your contact and payment details.
  5. Click **"Book Now"** to finalize the purchase and receive a success confirmation.
